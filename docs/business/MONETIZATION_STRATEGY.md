**Purpose**: Revenue models and pricing strategy  
**Author**: Business Team  
**Last Updated**: 2025-11-09  
**Status**: Active  

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Premium Subscriptions
- **Free Tier**: 3 games/day, ad-supported
- **Premium Tier**: $4.99/month or $49.99/year
- **Family Plan**: $9.99/month (up to 6 accounts)
- **Enterprise**: $199/month (50+ users)

### 2. Cosmetic Store
- Custom avatars and themes
- Badge frames and animations
- Non-pay-to-win cosmetics only

### 3. Advertising (Free Tier)
- Rewarded video ads
- Banner ads (non-intrusive)
- Target: $2-3 ARPU annually

### 4. Seasonal Battle Pass
- $9.99 per season (quarterly)
- Exclusive cosmetics and badges
- Target: 10-15% adoption
