# Mind Wars - Community Guidelines

**Purpose**: Rules and moderation policies  
**Last Updated**: 2025-11-09  
**Status**: Active  

---

## Core Values
- Respect for all ages (8-80+)
- Family-friendly environment
- Zero tolerance for harassment
- Inclusive and welcoming community

## Rules
1. **Be Respectful**: Treat others with kindness
2. **Stay Family-Friendly**: No profanity, adult content, or violence
3. **Play Fair**: No cheating or exploitation
4. **Protect Privacy**: Don't share personal information
5. **Report Issues**: Help keep community safe

## Enforcement
- **Warning**: First offense (minor)
- **Temporary Ban**: Repeat or moderate offenses (24h-7 days)
- **Permanent Ban**: Severe or repeated violations
