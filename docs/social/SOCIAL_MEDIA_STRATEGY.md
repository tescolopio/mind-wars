## Executive Summary

Mind Wars' social media strategy focuses on building a community around cognitive health, family connection, and friendly competition. Our multi-platform approach targets different demographics where they already spend time, with content that educates, entertains, and engages.

**Core Message**: "Stay Sharp Together, On Your Schedule"

**Target Platforms**:
1. **Facebook** - Primary (family-focused content)
2. **Instagram** - Secondary (visual, younger demographic)
3. **TikTok** - Growth (viral potential, Gen Z)
4. **LinkedIn** - B2B (corporate team-building)
5. **Twitter/X** - Community (real-time engagement)
6. **YouTube** - Educational (long-form content)

## Community Management

### Response Guidelines

**Response Time Targets**:
- Comments: Within 4 hours during business hours
- DMs: Within 1 hour (customer support), 4 hours (general)
- Mentions: Within 2 hours
- Negative feedback: Within 1 hour

**Tone & Voice**:
- Friendly and approachable
- Knowledgeable but not condescending
- Enthusiastic about cognitive health
- Family-friendly (no profanity)
- Empathetic to user concerns

**Response Templates**:
- Welcome new community members
- Thank users for feedback
- Address technical issues
- Encourage user-generated content
- Handle negative feedback professionally

### Moderation Policy

**Remove/Hide**:
- Spam and promotional content
- Offensive language and harassment
- Inappropriate images or videos
- False health claims
- Personal information sharing

**Ban/Block**:
- Repeated violations after warnings
- Coordinated harassment campaigns
- Impersonation accounts
- Malicious links or scams

**Escalate to Team**:
- Major technical issues reported
- Potential PR crises
- Legal concerns or threats
- Partnership inquiries

## Influencer Strategy

### Micro-Influencer Partnerships (0-100K followers)

**Target Niches**:
1. **Parenting** (mommy bloggers, daddy influencers)
2. **Education** (teachers, homeschool advocates)
3. **Gaming** (casual/family gaming creators)
4. **Seniors/Aging** (healthy aging advocates)
5. **Wellness** (mental health, brain health)

**Partnership Models**:
- Free premium subscriptions for honest reviews
- Sponsored posts ($100-500 depending on reach)
- Affiliate codes (10% commission on subscriptions)
- Co-created content (game challenges, tutorials)
- Ambassador program (monthly retainer for ongoing content)

**Expected ROI**: $5-10 per dollar spent (CAC of $0.50-1.00 per user acquired)

### Macro-Influencer Campaigns (100K-1M followers)

**Phase 2+ Strategy** (after proving micro-influencer ROI):
- Larger budget campaigns ($1000-5000 per post)
- Multi-platform deals (YouTube + Instagram + TikTok)
- Exclusive game access or features
- Co-branded content opportunities

## Paid Social Strategy

### Phase 1: Testing (Months 1-3, Budget: $5K/month)

**Objectives**:
- Test creative concepts
- Identify best-performing audiences
- Establish baseline CAC and conversion rates

**Platforms**:
- Facebook/Instagram: 70% of budget
- TikTok: 20% of budget
- Google App Campaigns: 10% of budget

**Audiences to Test**:
- Lookalike audiences (based on early users)
- Interest targeting (brain training, family activities, board games)
- Behavioral targeting (mobile gamers, app purchasers)
- Demographic targeting (parents, grandparents, professionals)

**Creative Formats**:
- Static image ads
- Video ads (15-30 seconds)
- Carousel ads (game showcases)
- User-generated content ads

**KPIs**:
- Cost per install (CPI): Target <$2
- Cost per registration: Target <$3
- Day 7 retention: Target >25%
- Cost per acquisition (paying user): Target <$50

### Phase 2: Scaling (Months 4-6, Budget: $20K/month)

**Objectives**:
- Scale winning campaigns
- Expand to new audiences
- Optimize for premium conversions

**Tactics**:
- Increase budgets on best-performing campaigns by 20-30% weekly
- Launch retargeting campaigns for engaged users
- Test premium subscription conversion campaigns
- Expand geographic targeting (international markets)

### Phase 3: Optimization (Months 7+, Budget: $50K+/month)

**Objectives**:
- Efficient scaling to mass market
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Premium tier optimization

**Tactics**:
- Programmatic buying for efficiency
- Brand awareness video campaigns
- Seasonal campaigns (holidays, back-to-school)
- Retargeting funnels optimized for premium conversion

## Crisis Management

### Potential Crises

**1. Negative Review Goes Viral**
- **Response**: Acknowledge quickly, offer solution, take conversation private
- **Prevention**: Monitor sentiment closely, address issues proactively

**2. Cognitive Training Claims Challenged**
- **Response**: Cite scientific backing, transparency about limitations
- **Prevention**: Clear disclaimers, partnership with scientists

**3. User Safety Issue (child safety, inappropriate content)**
- **Response**: Immediate action, transparent communication, policy updates
- **Prevention**: Strong moderation, safety features, clear guidelines

**4. Technical Outage During Peak Usage**
- **Response**: Rapid acknowledgment, transparent updates, compensation
- **Prevention**: Robust infrastructure, monitoring, communication plan

**5. Competitor Attacks or Negative Comparison**
- **Response**: Stay professional, highlight unique value, avoid direct confrontation
- **Prevention**: Build strong brand reputation, authentic community

### Crisis Response Protocol

1. **Acknowledge**: Respond within 1 hour acknowledging awareness
2. **Investigate**: Gather facts quickly, assess severity
3. **Communicate**: Provide transparent update within 4 hours
4. **Act**: Implement solution or mitigation
5. **Follow-up**: Update community on resolution
6. **Learn**: Conduct post-mortem, prevent future occurrences
